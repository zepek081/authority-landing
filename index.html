How Authority works

Three steps.
Clean, structured, deployable.

STEP 01 · EXTRACT
Full catalog. Both platforms. Every version.

Direct API query to Beatport v4 and Spotify. No manual exports. Returns every release, every version identifier, every ISRC across the artist's full history on both platforms. First run: complete back catalog. Subsequent runs: delta only.

→
STEP 02 · INJECT
Administrative data, mapped to every ISRC.

Your validated IPI/CAE codes, society affiliations, writer and publisher data — entered once per artist, then automatically propagated across every newly discovered ISRC. The platform's technical layer and your administrative layer, merged into a single coherent output.

→
STEP 03 · DEPLOY
Structured output. Import anywhere.

A clean Excel master sheet — every field already mapped. Import directly into your royalty management system, feed your CWR generator, or use as the reference for PRO registration. The data layer is complete. What you do with it is yours.

01
Every version tracked. Every claim supported.

Original mix, radio edit, extended, instrumental — each version carries a distinct ISRC and is a separately registerable work. Authority identifies and separates every version automatically, providing a complete basis for registration.

02
Incremental extraction. Process only new releases.

Every extracted release is stored locally. Each run compares the live catalog against your existing data and pulls only what's new. Your catalog grows in lock‑step with the artists you manage — minimizing redundancy and ensuring version consistency.

03
Remixes, tracked as distinct works.

Authority automatically identifies remixes, edits, radio versions and instrumentals and routes them to a dedicated sheet, correctly flagged. Each one is a potential alternative title — a separate registration, a separate royalty stream. They're handled automatically.

04
The Administrative Source of Truth. CWR and PRO ready.

The master sheet is not a report — it is the authoritative reference for every downstream workflow. ISRC, UPC, IPI, Society affiliation, split structure, release date — every field the CWR standard or PRO portal requires, already present and structured. When your royalty management system, your CWR generator, or your collecting society submission expects clean input, this is the file it receives. One source. No reconciliation required.

05
Your entire roster, processed in one run.

Upload a JSON with your full roster. Authority processes every artist in sequence — rate limiting, retry logic, and deduplication built in. One run to establish your baseline. Every run after that is incremental, targeted, and fast.

06
Back catalog recovery, fully filterable.

Artists who have never had a publisher, or who are changing management, often have years of releases with no formal administrative record. Authority maps the entire published history retroactively — filterable by release date to isolate contract windows, sortable by version type to flag unregistered works. Identify versions, remixes, and old releases that were never reported to your administration. Recovery starts here.

The origin

Authority was built out of direct operational experience — not to explain the problem, but to eliminate it.

Mosè Ruggiero has spent over a decade working inside music operations — with publishers, label managers, and catalog administrators, including Loose Club Edition. The catalog census was always the first bottleneck of every new relationship: manual, slow, and structurally incomplete. Authority applies enterprise API integration patterns to automate it entirely — the same class of problem, the domain he already knew from the inside.

The data layer is the foundation

Registration quality starts with the record behind it.

When a work is missing an ISRC, carries an incorrect version identifier, or has never been formally mapped — the gap surfaces downstream, during claims or reconciliation. Authority ensures the technical record is complete and correct from the first run, so every subsequent workflow operates on solid ground.
